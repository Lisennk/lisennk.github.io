Hi, my name is Taras Pohrebniak.

I'm a product manager, developer, and author.
Since 2017, my passion has been in AI and digital mental health.

My "Why"

In 2017, I went through a severe depression for the first time. I discovered that it's not one of those challenges that "make us stronger." It makes you weaker, without providing anything in return.

Overall, 67.5% of people who suffer from mental health issues do not get help. I want to build a solution to this problem and help everyone who fights the darkness.

Currently I am CEO at Elomia Health, where our long-term goal is to create an AI-powered companion that can provide therapy on a human level.

My Mental Health Products

Elomia Health ↗

An app with an AI-powered companion that listens, understands, and provides mental health support 24/7.

It was covered by many media outlets and mentioned by President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky. Another cool mention was made in the UK Parliament by Mira Murati, CTO at OpenAI.

DreamSpace ↗

In 2017 I founded an online marketplace that makes it easier and cheaper for people looking for a therapist to find one. The service matched therapists with patients and provided billing and telemedicine solutions.

My Other Products

LskaBots ↗

In 2015 started as my freelance project and turned out to be one of the first chatbot companies on the Eastern European market. We developed solutions for companies like BurgerKing and others. Our chatbots served more than 10 million users in 30 countries.

Poetry AI ↗

A pre-GPT era chatbot that allowed to create poetry. Started as a pet project and was acquired by a large entertainment service 2 months after launch.

/dev/null ↗

I owned the entertainment part of Tproger Media, and led the content production team. Over two years we built a community from zero to 200K members. Today, about two million people interact with our projects on a monthly basis.

Secret Chat ↗

I built a decentralized P2P messenger with 2048 bit RSA encryption. It works in browser and written in just 50 lines of JavaScript. No installation, no login, no databases. Just copy a repository from GitHub and you are ready to go.
